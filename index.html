Cusco, Peru

Peru is located on the west coast of South America. It encompasses the coastal desert, the Andes mountain range, and the Amazon rainforest. It is known for the Inca Empire and Machu Picchu. The official language is Spanish, although native languages such as Quechua are also spoken. Its rich culture is evident in its cuisine, landscapes, and the legacy of its ancient civilizations, such as the Nazca. Peru is also known for its unique cuisine. Cusco is its historic capital and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
